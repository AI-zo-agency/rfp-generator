## 2. RFP Proposal System (Claude Specialist)

> Sources: `00_Claude_KnowledgeBase_Reference.md`, Filing Guide, Writing Guide, Pricing Guide

### Core Operating Principle

**Never invent facts.** Every claim, metric, client name, and outcome must come from a verified file. If it isn't in the knowledge base, flag it — never fill gaps with assumptions.

### Five-Stage Workflow

Run stages in order. Do not skip unless explicitly requested.

| Stage | Trigger | Output |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1 — Fit Analysis** | "Run Stage 1" | Go/No-Go recommendation, compliance snapshot, capability assessment, scoring weights, competitive context |
| **Stage 2 — Structural Map** | "Run Stage 2" | RFP sections mapped to template, writing roadmap, page limits, team assignments |
| **Stage 3 — Write Proposal** | "Run Stage 3" | Full proposal content (Sections 4 & 5), labeled for designer, verified facts and pricing |
| **Stage 4 — Pre-Submission Review** | "Run Stage 4" | Compliance audit, scoring simulation, win probability, rewrite flags |
| **Stage 5 — Post-Award Learning** | "Run Stage 5" | Insights from scoring sheets/competitor proposals, KB update recommendations |

### Stage Details

#### Stage 1 — Fit Analysis
- Run within **24 hours** of RFP landing
- Owner: RFP writer (Rachel triages if unassigned)
- Outputs: AI Fit Score (0–5), Worth It Score (0–5), critical gaps, Go/No-Go/Revise
- **Gate:** No Stage 2 without explicit Go decision

#### Stage 2 — Compliance Mapping
- Map every requirement: mandatory docs, certifications, page limits, formatting, submission portal
- Build compliance checklist before any writing
- Target **48-hour buffer** before submission deadline
- **Gate:** All mandatory documents confirmed (Have/Need/N/A) before writing begins

#### Stage 3 — Strategic Drafting
- **Claude writes Sections 4 and 5 only** (project approach + scope)
- Sections 1–3 (Company Overview, Team Bios, Case Studies) come from master template
- Select **2–4 case studies** by sector match, scope similarity, audience alignment
- Use approved pricing from Pricing Guide

#### Stage 4 — Pre-Submission Review
- Sonja: strategic positioning
- Curt: design and brand consistency
- Internal deadline: **48 hours before** actual submission

#### Stage 5 — Submission & Post-Award
- Archive in Google Drive
- FOIA analysis if applicable
- Upload scoring sheets as `09_SCORE_` files

### Voice Standards

- Direct, confident, warm — not corporate
- Active voice, declarative statements
- Client-outcome focused, not process-focused
- No em dashes
- No "it's not just X, it's Y" constructions
- No filler: "it's worth noting," "in today's landscape," "robust," "seamlessly," lazy "leverage"
- No defensive over-explaining
- Aspirational, not fear-based
- Every section must feel written specifically for this client

### Writer's Review Pass (5 Passes)

1. **Voice & Tone** — Read aloud; strip AI patterns
2. **Specificity** — Client-specific references; cut generic statements
3. **Verification** — Trace every claim to verified source
4. **Compliance** — Check against Stage 2 compliance map
5. **Differentiation** — Clear big idea; compelling reason to pick zö
