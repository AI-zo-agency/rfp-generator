# Zo Agency Apollo Mind Map

## RFP Process
1. Set up knowledge base — import facts, pricing, bios, case studies, won proposals, and guides
2. RFP intake from JustWin — detect new RFPs, pull metadata and PDF, create an active record
3. Go / no-go analysis — parse RFP, score fit and worth-it, recommend bid or pass for approval
4. Compliance mapping — build checklist and outline with sections, page limits, and required questions
5. Sections 1–3 — auto-fill company overview, bios, and case studies from the knowledge base
6. Sections 4–5 — draft custom approach and scope using RFP context and the Writing Guide
7. Pricing assistant — suggest line items, tiers, and qualifying language from the Pricing Guide
8. Pre-submission review — run voice, verification, and compliance checks before submit
9. Export manuscript — output design-ready content for zö's design team to lay out and submit

## Financial Dashboard
1. One live view of revenue, project hours, and costs
2. Spend vs budget tracking on every active project
3. Auto-flag billing errors and unusual transactions
4. Catch scope creep before projects run over
5. Track revenue and profit against agency goals
6. AI insights — weekly brief on risks, trends, and wins
7. Ask your finances — AI answers questions over live data

## Lead Finding & Outreach
1. Research prospects that match zö's sectors and client profile
2. Score lead fit before the team spends time on outreach
3. Pull context on each prospect — industry, size, recent news
4. Draft personalized first-touch emails in zö's voice
5. Suggest follow-up timing and next steps per lead
6. Track who's been contacted and current response status
7. Log outreach in the pipeline so nothing falls through
8. Flag municipal and government opportunities worth pursuing
9. Draft intro messages for warm LinkedIn or referral connections
10. Surface relevant past wins and case studies per prospect
